# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on end-to-end user experience, ensuring that products are intuitive, accessible, and meet user needs. They collaborate with Product Managers, Developers, and QA to validate designs and ensure usability standards are met.

### Responsibilities
- Design user interfaces and interaction flows based on user research
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Conduct usability testing and gather user feedback
- Ensure accessibility and inclusive design standards are met
- Collaborate with developers to implement designs accurately
- Partner with Product Managers to align designs with business goals

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive and delightful user experiences
- Ensure accessibility compliance and inclusive design
- Reduce user friction and support tickets through better design
- Validate design decisions through user research and testing

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- User research findings and usability test reports
- Design handoff documentation and component libraries
- Weekly sync with product and engineering on feature designs

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers build and maintain CI/CD pipelines, automate infrastructure, and ensure reliable software delivery. They work closely with Developers and QA to enable seamless deployment, monitoring, and observability.

### Responsibilities
- Design and maintain CI/CD pipelines for automated builds and deployments
- Manage cloud infrastructure and container orchestration
- Implement monitoring, logging, and alerting systems
- Automate infrastructure provisioning and configuration management
- Ensure system reliability, scalability, and performance
- Support incident response and post-mortem analysis

### Goals
- Minimize deployment friction and lead time
- Achieve high system uptime and reliability
- Enable fast feedback loops through automation
- Improve observability and incident response times

### Typical Communication
- Daily coordination with Developers on build and deployment issues
- Infrastructure change proposals and architecture reviews
- Incident reports and post-mortem documentation
- Sprint planning input on infrastructure and tooling work

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads coordinate test strategy, coverage, and quality assurance activities across the team. They work closely with Developers, Product Managers, and Project Managers to ensure release readiness and quality targets are met.

### Responsibilities
- Define and maintain test strategy and quality standards
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Review test coverage and identify quality gaps
- Lead bug triage and prioritization discussions
- Facilitate QA involvement in sprint planning and reviews
- Track quality metrics and report on release readiness

### Goals
- Ensure comprehensive test coverage across features
- Detect defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain high quality standards for releases
- Reduce production incidents through effective testing

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning with development team
- Quality gate reviews and release readiness sign-offs
- Bug reports and test result summaries
- Weekly metrics on test coverage and defect trends

---

## Security Champion

### Role Summary
Security Champions advocate and implement security practices throughout the project lifecycle. They coordinate security reviews, vulnerability assessments, and incident responses in partnership with Developers, DevOps, and Product Managers.

### Responsibilities
- Promote security best practices and secure coding standards
- Conduct security reviews and threat modeling sessions
- Coordinate vulnerability scanning and remediation efforts
- Lead security incident response and investigation
- Provide security training and guidance to team members
- Ensure compliance with security policies and regulations

### Goals
- Integrate security early in the development lifecycle
- Minimize security vulnerabilities in production
- Improve team security awareness and practices
- Maintain compliance with security standards and regulations

### Typical Communication
- Security review sessions during design and code reviews
- Vulnerability reports and remediation plans
- Security incident notifications and post-mortems
- Monthly security metrics and compliance updates

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts gather, clarify, and document requirements, translating business needs into actionable backlog items. They collaborate across teams to ensure clear understanding of requirements and help bridge the gap between stakeholders and technical teams.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and analyze business requirements from stakeholders
- Document functional and non-functional requirements
- Create process flows, use cases, and acceptance criteria
- Facilitate requirements workshops and clarification sessions
- Validate that delivered features meet business needs
- Maintain traceability between requirements and deliverables

### Goals
- Ensure clear, well-documented requirements
- Reduce ambiguity and rework due to misunderstood requirements
- Bridge communication between business and technical teams
- Support data-driven decision making with analysis

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders and team
- Backlog refinement sessions with Product Managers and Developers
- Requirement documentation and user stories
- Weekly status on requirements clarity and readiness

---

## Customer Success

### Role Summary
Customer Success professionals channel end-user feedback to the project team, supporting post-release improvements and customer onboarding. They help adapt the product based on real-world usage and pain points, working closely with Product Managers.

### Responsibilities
- Collect and synthesize customer feedback and feature requests
- Support customer onboarding and training initiatives
- Track customer satisfaction and adoption metrics
- Escalate critical customer issues to the development team
- Provide product insights based on customer interactions
- Facilitate customer advisory boards and feedback sessions

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and product adoption
- Reduce churn through proactive support and engagement
- Ensure customer feedback informs product decisions
- Improve onboarding and time-to-value for new customers

### Typical Communication
- Weekly feedback summaries shared with Product Managers
- Customer escalation reports and urgent issue notifications
- Monthly insights on customer trends and feature requests
- Quarterly business reviews with key customers and internal teams
